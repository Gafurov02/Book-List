Book List

1984

Author: George Orwell

Description: A dystopian social science fiction novel and cautionary tale, warning of the dangers of totalitarianism and extreme political ideology.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Description: A novel about the serious issues of rape and racial inequality, it is also renowned for its warmth and humor.

Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Description: A romantic novel that charts the emotional development of the protagonist, Elizabeth Bennet, as she learns about the repercussions of hasty judgments and comes to appreciate the difference between superficial goodness and actual goodness.

The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Description: A novel about the decline of the American Dream in the 1920s, it portrays the young and mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his quixotic passion for the beautiful Daisy Buchanan.

Moby Dick

Author: Herman Melville

Description: The narrative of Captain Ahab's obsessive quest to kill the giant white whale Moby Dick, which had previously bitten off Ahab's leg at the knee.
